## Core

- Ultracal 30 (10 lb)
- Black iron oxide (Fe3O4) ~500 g
- Xanthan gum (food grade) 6 oz
- Superplasticizer (polycarboxylate), ~1 lb
- Fine sand 40–80 mesh (optional)

## Acoustics

- Fosi TB10D (TPA3255) + included PSU
- Dayton TT25-8 tactile transducer (×1; optional ×2 for later)
- 3.5 mm → RCA cable; rubber feet; epoxy (transducer mount)

## Magnetics

- Neodymium bar magnets 60×10×3 mm (pack of 24)

## Fabrication

- PETG/ABS for tray or machine from acrylic/aluminum
- Silicone sealant (thin bead at tray corners)
- 3M VHB LSE tape or epoxy for temporary transducer bonding

## Lab & Safety

- Disposable LDPE transfer pipettes (3–5 mL pack)
- Nitrile gloves, respirator, and safety glasses/ear protection for mixing
